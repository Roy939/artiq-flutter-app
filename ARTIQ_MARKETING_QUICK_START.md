### Week 1: Foundation & Setup

**Day 1-2: Social Media Setup**
- Create accounts on: TikTok, Instagram, Twitter/X, Pinterest, Reddit

- Profile bio: "Design made simple. Create stunning graphics in minutes. Free forever. üé® artiq.works"

**Day 3-4: Content Preparation**
- Create 10 "before and after" design transformation videos (30-60 seconds each)
- Film 5 quick tutorials: "How to make an Instagram post in 30 seconds"
- Design 20 sample templates to showcase in content
- Prepare captions and hashtags for each post

**Day 5-7: Launch Prep**
- Write Product Hunt launch description
- Create launch video (2-3 minutes)
- Prepare 5 GIFs showing key features
- Set up email capture on artiq.works
- Create launch day special offer (e.g., "First 1000 users get 3 months Pro free")

### Week 2: Launch Week

**Product Hunt Launch**
- Post on Product Hunt Tuesday-Thursday (best days)
- Engage with every comment within 1 hour
- Share launch on all social media
- Ask friends/family to upvote and comment

**Social Media Blitz**
- Post 2x daily on TikTok/Instagram (morning & evening)
- Share to Pinterest (5 pins daily)
- Post in relevant Reddit communities (1-2 per day, be helpful not spammy)
- Tweet 3-5x daily with design tips and ARTIQ features

**Outreach**
- Email 20 design/marketing bloggers with free Pro access
- DM 10 micro-influencers (10K-50K followers) on Instagram
- Post in Facebook groups for small business owners

### Week 3: Content Engine

**Blog Posts (2 per week)**
- "10 Free Instagram Post Templates for Small Businesses"
- "How to Design a Logo Without Being a Designer"

**Video Content (10 videos)**
- Quick tutorials for TikTok/Instagram Reels
- Focus on trending topics and challenges

**Template Creation**
- Add 50 new templates across popular categories
- Optimize each template page for SEO

### Week 4: Community & Feedback

**Engage with Users**
- Reply to every comment/message within 24 hours
- Ask for feedback and feature requests
- Create a Discord or Facebook group for ARTIQ users

**User-Generated Content**
- Run a contest: "Share your ARTIQ design for a chance to win 1 year Pro"
- Repost user designs (with permission)
- Feature "Design of the Week" on social media

## üéØ Target Platforms by Priority

### High Priority (Start Here)

**1. TikTok**
- Fastest growing platform
- Great for viral growth
- Young audience (18-34)
- Post 2x daily

**2. Instagram**
- Visual platform perfect for design
- Reels have high reach
- Stories for daily engagement
- Post 1-2x daily

**3. Pinterest**
- High intent users searching for templates
- Long-lasting content (pins live forever)
- Great for SEO and backlinks
- Pin 5-10x daily

### Medium Priority (Launch After Week 2)

**4. YouTube**
- Longer tutorials (5-10 minutes)
- SEO benefits
- Build authority
- Post 1x weekly

**5. Twitter/X**
- Engage with design community
- Share quick tips
- Customer support
- Tweet 3-5x daily

### Low Priority (Launch After Month 1)

**6. LinkedIn**
- B2B marketing
- Professional audience
- Post 2-3x weekly

**7. Facebook**
- Older demographic
- Groups are valuable
- Post 1x daily

## üí∞ Paid Advertising (When You Have Budget)

### Start Small: $500-1000/month

**Facebook/Instagram Ads**
- Target: Small business owners, marketers, content creators
- Age: 25-45
- Interests: Canva, graphic design, social media marketing
- Ad format: Video showing ARTIQ in action
- Objective: Free sign-ups

**Google Ads (Search)**
- Keywords: "free design tool," "Canva alternative," "easy graphic design"
- Landing page: artiq.works homepage
- Budget: $20-30/day

**TikTok Ads**
- Promote your best-performing organic videos
- Target: 18-35, interests in design, marketing, small business
- Budget: $10-20/day
