This Privacy Policy explains how ARTIQ ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile and web application ("the App").

By using ARTIQ, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

When you create an account, we collect:

- Password (encrypted)
- Display name (if provided)
- Profile picture (if provided)

**Payment Information**
When you subscribe to Pro features:

- We receive a customer ID and subscription status from Stripe

**Content You Create**
- Designs and artwork you create in the App

**Communications**
- Support requests and correspondence

### 2.2 Information Automatically Collected

**Usage Data**
- Features you use and how often
- Time spent in the App
- Actions performed (creating, editing, deleting designs)
- Subscription status and tier

**Device Information**
- Device type and model
- Operating system and version
- App version
- Unique device identifiers
- IP address
- Browser type (for web version)

**Analytics Data**
- App performance metrics
- Crash reports and error logs
- Feature usage statistics

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

**Authentication Providers**
When you sign in with Google:
- We receive your name, email, and profile picture from Google
- This is governed by Google's privacy policy

**Payment Processor**
Stripe provides us with:
- Payment status
- Customer ID
- Subscription status
- We do not receive your full payment card details

### 5.1 Where We Store Data
- Data is stored on Firebase servers (Google Cloud Platform)
- Servers are located in the United States
- Data may be transferred internationally

### 5.2 Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption at rest for sensitive data
- Secure authentication (Firebase Auth)
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and monitoring

### 5.3 Data Retention
- Account data is retained while your account is active
